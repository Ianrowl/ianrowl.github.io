Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

The first accessibility concern that might be present to viewers of this website is the chessboard. Those who rely on screen readers to navigate and read through websites may not be able to get an accurate representation of the chessboard that's built on the screen. The best way to address this would be to have an alt text or subheading that explains what is shown in the chessboard. Another thing that may be an accessibility concern are the labels for the Challenge 3 form as well as the submit button. As of now, my submit button simply refreshes the page rather than submitting the answers somewhere so that may confuse many viewers. The way to these concerns would be to have an actual place that the data is submitted to, and maybe better labelling.
