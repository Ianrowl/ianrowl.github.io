More about me.

I grew up in Denver, Colorado and was a student at Denver Waldorf School up until attending Littleton High School. My education has played a large role in the person I have become. In my early schooling, I was encouraged to be creative and imaginative with everything I did, and in high school, I was a part of the International Baccalaureate Program, a rigorous program which taught me many technical skills as well as improved my preparation for college and work tremendously.

Some passions of mine include music and music production, creating visual art, and sports.
